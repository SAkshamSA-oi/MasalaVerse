Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 medium onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

Step 1: In a bowl, mix chicken with curd, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder, and salt. Marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight gives best results).

Step 2: Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add tomato puree, garam masala, and cook until oil separates from the gravy.

Step 3: Add marinated chicken and cook on medium flame until tender and cooked through. Stir occasionally.

Step 4: Pour in fresh cream and sprinkle kasuri methi. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 5: Garnish with a little butter and coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan or basmati rice.
