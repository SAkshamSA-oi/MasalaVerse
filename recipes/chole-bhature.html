Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled chickpeas (chole)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Bhature Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 4 tbsp yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Water (to knead)
  • Oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saute onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas and a little water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Mix flour, yogurt, baking soda, and knead into a dough. Rest 20 minutes.
  6. Roll into discs and deep fry until puffed and golden.
  7. Serve hot with the prepared chole.
