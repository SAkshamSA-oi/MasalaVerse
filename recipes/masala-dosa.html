Ingredients (Dosa Batter)

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients (Potato Filling)

  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Green chili + curry leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak rice and dal for 6 hours, grind and ferment overnight.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, onions, turmeric & salt. Add potatoes.
  3. Spread batter on a hot tawa to make a thin dosa.
  4. Add filling and fold. Serve with sambhar and chutney.
